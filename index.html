OUR MISSION

CELEBRATING CINEMA & CREATIVE STORYTELLING

At Film Club REC, we are passionate about cinema as an art form that transcends boundaries, sparks imagination, and inspires thought. We aim to create a vibrant community where film enthusiasts can explore, discuss, and create compelling visual narratives.

  • Screening and Analyzing Classic & Contemporary Films
  • Workshops on Filmmaking, Screenwriting & Editing
